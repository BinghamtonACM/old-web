Programming Competition Sponsored by Bloomberg

Get ready for ACM's first programming competition of the semester!

RSVP here at our Facebook event.

Location is at AA G004, computers will be available but you may compete on your own laptops.

Food and refreshments will be served at 8:30 pm and the competition will officially begin around 9:10 and last until 11:10.

Competition questions cater to absolute beginners as well as seasoned experts!

Rules:

  • No teams, solo competition
  • Basically any language you'd like
  • Rankings by number correct and time submitted
  • Two hour time limit

There will be prizes for 1st, 2nd, and 3rd, as well as freshman prizes.

The ACM board is looking to send teams to the ICPC in November. We will be consulting the results of this competition as well as the Microsoft competition last week.

Bloomberg representatives will be at the competition!
